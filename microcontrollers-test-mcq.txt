1. What is the program storage space (ROM) size in the standard 8051 microcontroller?
a) 2 KB
b) 4 KB
c) 8 KB
d) 16 KB
Correct Answer: B

2. How many 16-bit timers/counters does the standard 8051 microcontroller have?
a. One
b. Two
c. Three
d. Four
ANS: B

Q: Which of the following registers is used as the Program Status Word in 8051?
A) DPTR
B) SP
C) PSW
D) PC
Answer: C

[4] What is the word size of the 8051 microcontroller's internal RAM?
A. 4 bits
B. 8 bits
C. 16 bits
D. 32 bits
ANS: B

A) RST (Pin 9)
